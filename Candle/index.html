Candle

2D lighting for SFML

Candle is an extension for SFML v2.5.1, written in C++ to provide light effects, shadow casting and field of view functionalities with easy integration.

Features

  • Dynamic shadow casting.
  • Flexible light types.
  • Areas with fog, optionally textured.
  • No additional dependencies.
  • Ready-to-use demo program.
